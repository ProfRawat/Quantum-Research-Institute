Exploring the Frontiers of Quantum Research

The Quantum Research Institute advances transformative research in quantum computing, communications, cybersecurity, sensing, and emerging technologies through interdisciplinary collaboration and innovation.

Message from the Director

“Welcome to the Quantum Research Institute. Our mission is to foster pioneering research and workforce development in quantum science and engineering while building collaborations that drive innovation and national impact. Together, we are shaping the next generation of quantum technologies and scientific discovery.”


Dr. DBR

Institute Director

Research Excellence

Advancing research and innovation in quantum computing & cybersecurity, quantum machine learning, quantum communications & networking, quantum sensing technologies (for RF and PNT), quantum noise & decoherence mitigation and quantum error-correction methods.

Workforce Development

Recruiting, retaining, mentoring, and training future researchers, engineers, and innovators through interdisciplinary research and education, while preparing a highly skilled workforce in emerging quantum systems and technologies.

Global Collaboration

Building strategic partnerships with academia, industry, and government to accelerate the development, translation, and deployment of quantum innovations, while fostering collaborative research and technology transfer across the quantum ecosystem.
